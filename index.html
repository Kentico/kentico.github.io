Kontent Discord

Talk about Kontent, be up-to-date on interesting feature releases, events, and everything else that happens around Kontent.

Feel free to join the Kontent DevRel team, all Kontent MVPs, and Kentico staff and discuss anything Kontent related on Discord.

Communicate in channels and directly. Choose audio, video, or text when you feel like it. Distinguishable personas let you know exactly who you're talking to. Be promptly informed about releases, events, and other news, and let others know about you and your projects.
